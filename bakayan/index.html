Bakkayan Beach Resort

Located along the pristine coastline of Unit 3, Dinagsaan, Puntalinao, Banay-banay, Philippines, our resort beckons you to indulge in the ultimate beach getaway. With powder-soft sands, crystal-clear waters, and a serene atmosphere, Bakkayan Beach Resort is your sanctuary for relaxation and adventure. Join us as we embark on a journey of sun, sand, and endless moments of bliss at Bakkayan Beach Resort.

  • Indulge in relaxation at our heated outdoor pool and whirlpool
  • Embark on coastal explorations with our guided tours or venture out on your own
  • Begin your day with a complimentary cooked-to-order breakfast
  • Unwind in our lodge with complimentary appetizers and beverages
  • Experience nightly fine dining with breathtaking ocean views
