About Us

Welcome to Bakkayan Beach Resort, where tranquility meets adventure. Our resort is nestled along the pristine coastline of Unit 3, Dinagsaan, Puntalinao, Banay-banay, Philippines. Here, you'll discover the perfect blend of relaxation and excitement.

At Bakkayan Beach Resort, we're dedicated to providing our guests with unforgettable experiences. Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat by the sea or thrilling outdoor activities, we have something for everyone.

Our team is committed to delivering exceptional service and ensuring that your stay with us is nothing short of extraordinary. From our luxurious accommodations to our world-class amenities, every detail is crafted with your comfort and satisfaction in mind.

Come and explore the wonders of Bakkayan Beach Resort. Let us be your gateway to paradise.
